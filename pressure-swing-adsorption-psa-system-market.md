<p><p>A Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) System is a gas separation technology used to separate a specific gas from a mixture of gases under pressure. This system operates on the principle of adsorption, where molecules of a gas are selectively adsorbed onto the surface of a solid adsorbent material while the rest of the gas passes through. By cycling between high pressure adsorption and low pressure desorption, the desired gas is concentrated and purified.</p><p>The Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA) System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.8% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for PSA systems in various industries such as healthcare, food and beverage, and manufacturing is a key driver for market growth. The growing industrialization and stringent government regulations on emissions are further propelling the market growth.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in PSA technology, such as the development of more efficient adsorbents and improved system design, are driving innovation in the market.
