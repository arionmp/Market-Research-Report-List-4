There are two main types of electric energy meters based on the number of phases they measure - single-phase and three-phase electric energy meters. Single-phase meters are typically used in residential buildings where the electrical system consists of a single phase, while three-phase meters are used in commercial and industrial buildings with three-phase electrical systems. Both types of meters help in accurate billing and efficient energy management.</p></p>
