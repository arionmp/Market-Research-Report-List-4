<p><p>The Silicon Controlled Rectifiers (SCR) market consists of two main types - Unidirectional SCR and Bidirectional SCR. Unidirectional SCRs conduct current in only one direction, making them suitable for applications where current flow is only needed in one direction. On the other hand, Bidirectional SCRs can conduct current in both directions, allowing for more versatile use in applications where current flow needs to be controlled in both directions. Both types of SCRs play a vital role in regulating and controlling power in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
