<p><p>Surge arresters are used to protect electrical systems from voltage spikes. There are two main types of surge arresters in the market: polymeric and porcelain. Polymeric surge arresters are made of synthetic materials like silicone rubber, offering advantages such as lightweight, compact design, and improved pollution performance. Porcelain surge arresters, on the other hand, are made of traditional ceramic material and are known for their high mechanical strength and long service life. Both types of surge arresters are widely used in various industries to safeguard electrical equipment from damage caused by surges in voltage.</p></p>
