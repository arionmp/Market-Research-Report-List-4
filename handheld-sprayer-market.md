<p><p>Handheld sprayers are available in two main types: Engine Drive Sprayer and Motor Drive Sprayer. Engine Drive Sprayers are powered by small engines, typically gas-powered, providing higher spraying power and coverage for larger areas. On the other hand, Motor Drive Sprayers are powered by electric motors, offering quieter operation and suitability for indoor use. Both types are commonly used for tasks such as spraying pesticides, fertilizers, and cleaning solutions in agricultural, gardening, and industrial settings.</p></p>
