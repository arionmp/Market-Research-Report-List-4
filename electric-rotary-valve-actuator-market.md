<p><p>Electric Rotary Valve Actuator market has two main types based on the connection to the valve: Directly Attached Type and Pedestal Type. In Directly Attached Type, the actuator is directly attached to the valve, providing a compact and efficient solution for smaller valves. In Pedestal Type, the actuator is mounted on a separate pedestal next to the valve, offering flexibility in installation and maintenance for larger valves. Both types cater to different valve sizes and applications, meeting the diverse needs of industries requiring rotary valve automation.</p></p>
