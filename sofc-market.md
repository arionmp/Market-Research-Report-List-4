<p><p>SOFC market is divided into three main types: Planar, Tubular, and Others. Tubular SOFCs have cylindrical cells arranged in a tube-like structure, providing better durability and performance at high temperatures. Other types include flat-tubular and monolithic designs that offer unique advantages. Each type has its own characteristics and applications, catering to diverse needs in various industries.</p></p>
