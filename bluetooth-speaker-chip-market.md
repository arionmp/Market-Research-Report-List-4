<p><p>The Bluetooth speaker chip market is divided into two main types: Classic Bluetooth chip and BLE chip. Classic Bluetooth chips are used in traditional Bluetooth speakers and devices, providing high-quality audio streaming over a longer range. On the other hand, BLE chips are used in newer Bluetooth speakers and devices, offering lower power consumption and easier connectivity with smartphones and other devices. Both types of chips cater to different market needs, with Classic Bluetooth chips being more suited for audio quality and range, while BLE chips focus on energy efficiency and connectivity.</p></p>
