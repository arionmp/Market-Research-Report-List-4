<p><p>Rotary Pneumatic Actuator market consists of two main types: Double Acting Pneumatic Actuators and Single Acting Pneumatic Actuators. Double Acting Pneumatic Actuators use air pressure to open and close a valve, providing precise control over the process. On the other hand, Single Acting Pneumatic Actuators use air pressure to open the valve, while a spring mechanism closes it when the air pressure is released. Both types offer different levels of control and versatility, catering to various industrial applications.</p></p>
