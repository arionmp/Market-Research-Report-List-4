<p><p>Electronic metering pumps are categorized into various types based on their design and functioning, including diaphragm pumps, peristaltic pumps, and others. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to displace the liquid, providing accurate and consistent flow rates. Peristaltic pumps function by compressing a flexible tube to push the liquid forward, ensuring gentle and precise fluid delivery. Other types of electronic metering pumps may include piston pumps, gear pumps, or syringe pumps, each offering unique benefits depending on the application requirements.</p></p>
