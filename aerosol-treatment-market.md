<p><p>The aerosol treatment market comprises of different types of devices used for respiratory medication delivery. Metered Dose Inhalers (MDIs) are pressurized devices that deliver a specific amount of medication in each actuation. Dry Powder Inhalers (DPIs) deliver medication in powder form and require the patient to inhale rapidly to dispense the medication. Nebulizers are devices that convert liquid medication into a mist for inhalation. Other devices in the market include soft mist inhalers and Respimat inhalers, among others.</p></p>
