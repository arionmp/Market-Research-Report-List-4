<p><p>Elastic pin couplings are classified into three main types based on the shape of the shaft hole: Cylindrical Shaft Hole, Tapered Shaft Hole, and Short Cylindrical Shaft Hole. The Cylindrical Shaft Hole coupling is designed for applications requiring precise alignment and high torque transmission. The Tapered Shaft Hole coupling allows for easy installation and removal of the coupling from the shaft. The Short Cylindrical Shaft Hole coupling is suitable for compact spaces where a shorter overall length is required. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in the market.</p></p>
