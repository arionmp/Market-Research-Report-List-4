There are two main types of Air Entrainment Meters in the market: Water Column Type and Pressure Gauge Type. Water Column Type meters use a water column to measure air content, while Pressure Gauge Type meters utilize pressure changes in air bubbles to determine air content. Both types of meters are essential for ensuring the proper air content in concrete mixes, which can affect the durability and workability of the final product.</p></p>
