<p><p>Slit valves are commonly categorized into three market types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric. Hydraulic slit valves utilize fluid pressure to regulate flow, offering high precision and power in demanding environments. Electric slit valves are controlled using electrical signals, providing precise and reliable performance in sensitive applications.
