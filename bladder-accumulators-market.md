<p><p>Bladder accumulators are commonly categorized into two types: standard/high pressure bladder accumulators and low pressure bladder accumulators. Standard/high pressure bladder accumulators are designed to operate at higher pressures, typically ranging from 3000 to 6000 PSI, making them suitable for applications where high pressure fluid storage is required. On the other hand, low pressure bladder accumulators are designed for lower pressure applications, usually below 1000 PSI. Both types of bladder accumulators function by storing fluid under pressure, providing energy storage and hydraulic shock absorption in various industrial applications.</p></p>
