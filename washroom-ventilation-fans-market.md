<p><p>Washroom ventilation fans come in three main types: ceiling fans, wall-mounted fans, and window-mounted fans. Ceiling fans are installed on the ceiling and are ideal for larger washrooms. Wall-mounted fans are placed on the wall and are suitable for smaller washrooms with limited ceiling space. Window-mounted fans are installed on the window frame and are a good option for washrooms without proper ventilation. Each type of fan helps to remove excess moisture and odors, improving air quality in the washroom.</p></p>
