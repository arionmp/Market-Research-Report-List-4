<p><p>Thermal printing is a digital printing technique that produces high-quality images by selectively heating coated thermochromic paper or thermal media. The key advantages of thermal printing include faster printing speeds, quieter operation, lower maintenance costs, and high print quality.</p><p>The thermal printing market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 6.1% during the forecast period.
