<p><ul><li>Mechanical Damper</li><li>Pneumatic Damper</li><li>Hydraulic Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sliding door damper market consists of three main types: mechanical dampers, pneumatic dampers, and hydraulic dampers. Mechanical dampers use physical mechanisms to slow down the door's movement, while pneumatic dampers utilize compressed air to control the door's speed. Hydraulic dampers, on the other hand, use fluid power to provide resistance when opening and closing the door. Each type has its own advantages and is suited for different applications based on factors such as door weight, size, and desired speed control.</p></p>
