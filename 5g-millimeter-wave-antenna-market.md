<p><p>The 5G millimeter wave antenna market consists of two main types: Antenna on Chip (AoC) and Antenna in Package (AiP). AoC involves integrating the antenna directly onto a chip, allowing for a compact design and increased performance. AiP, on the other hand, involves placing the antenna within a package separate from the chip. AiP offers flexibility in terms of design and optimization. Both AoC and AiP play a crucial role in enabling the development and deployment of 5G technology.</p></p>
