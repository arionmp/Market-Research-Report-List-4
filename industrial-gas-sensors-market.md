<p><p>Industrial gas sensors are devices used to monitor and detect the presence of various gases in industrial settings. There are several types of gas sensors available in the market, including electrochemical, semiconductor, infrared, and photoionization detectors (PID). Electrochemical sensors work by measuring chemical reactions, semiconductor sensors detect gases through changes in electrical conductivity, infrared sensors detect gases based on their absorption of infrared light, and PID sensors use ultraviolet light to ionize gas molecules for detection. Each type of sensor offers unique advantages depending on the specific industrial application.</p></p>
