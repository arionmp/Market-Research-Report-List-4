<p><p>High pressure ball valves are available in two main types: remote mounting and direct mounting. </p><p>Remote mounting ball valves are installed away from the pipeline and are connected through tubing or piping, allowing for easy maintenance and repairs without disturbing the main pipeline. </p><p>Direct mounting ball valves, on the other hand, are directly mounted onto the pipelines, eliminating the need for additional connections and reducing the risk of leaks. </p><p>Both types of ball valves are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and power generation.</p></p>
