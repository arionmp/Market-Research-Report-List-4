<p><p>The commercial car tachograph market includes two main types: integrated and portable. Integrated tachographs are built-in systems installed directly into the vehicle, providing continuous monitoring of driving activities. Portable tachographs, on the other hand, are external devices that can be easily removed and transferred between vehicles. Integrated tachographs are typically preferred for fleets with dedicated vehicles, while portable tachographs offer flexibility for companies with multiple vehicles or temporary transportation needs. Both types provide essential data on driving performance and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
