<p><strong>The Advanced CO2 Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR CO2 Sensor</li><li>Chemical CO2 Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Advanced CO2 sensors market comprises of two main types: NDIR CO2 sensors and Chemical CO2 sensors. NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) sensors use infrared light to measure CO2 levels, providing high accuracy and stability. On the other hand, Chemical CO2 sensors utilize a chemical reaction to detect CO2, offering lower cost options but with potentially less accuracy. Both types are widely used in various industries such as HVAC, automotive, healthcare, and industrial applications for monitoring indoor air quality, safety, and environmental compliance.</p></p>
