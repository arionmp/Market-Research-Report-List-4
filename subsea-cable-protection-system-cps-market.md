There are two main types of CPS in the market: Metal Cable Protection Systems and Composite Material Cable Protection Systems. Metal CPS offer robust protection against mechanical threats but can be susceptible to corrosion. On the other hand, Composite Material CPS provide a lightweight and corrosion-resistant solution, making them ideal for harsh underwater environments. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the longevity and reliability of subsea cables.</p></p>
