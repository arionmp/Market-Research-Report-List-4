<p><p>Colorectal Cancer Diagnostics Market includes various types of tests such as Fecal Occult Blood (FOB) tests, Circulating Tumor Cell (CTC) tests, Carcinoembryonic Antigen (CEA) tests, and others. FOB tests involve detecting blood in stool samples, CTC tests identify cancer cells in the bloodstream, and CEA tests measure the levels of a protein that may be elevated in colorectal cancer. Other tests may include genetic or molecular diagnostics. These different types of tests play a crucial role in detecting and diagnosing colorectal cancer at different stages.</p></p>
