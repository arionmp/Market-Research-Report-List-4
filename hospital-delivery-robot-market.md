<p><p>Hospital Delivery Robots are classified into two main types based on their navigation systems: Magnet Navigation and Laser Navigation. Magnet Navigation robots use magnetic strips placed on the floor to determine their path, enabling precise and accurate navigation within hospital environments. Both types offer efficient and reliable delivery service within hospital settings, improving workflow and patient care.</p></p>
