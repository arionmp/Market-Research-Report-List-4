<p><p>Gene synthesis software market includes three main types - Solid Phase Synthesis, Chip-based DNA Synthesis, and PCR-based Enzyme Synthesis. Solid Phase Synthesis involves assembling DNA strands on solid support materials. Chip-based DNA Synthesis utilizes microarrays to synthesize genes in high-throughput manner. PCR-based Enzyme Synthesis uses polymerase chain reaction to amplify gene sequences. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in gene synthesis, catering to different research needs and preferences in the biotechnology industry.</p></p>
