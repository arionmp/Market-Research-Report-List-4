<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineering projectors are available in two main types: DLP (digital light processing) and LCD (liquid crystal display). DLP projectors use a chip made up of millions of tiny mirrors to reflect light and create an image, providing high contrast and color accuracy. On the other hand, LCD projectors use liquid crystal panels to project light through color filters, offering bright images and excellent sharpness. Both types have their strengths and cater to different needs in the market, making them popular choices in the engineering sector.</p></p>
