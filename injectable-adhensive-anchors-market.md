<p><p>Injectable adhesive anchors are widely used in the construction industry for anchoring bolts, rods, and rebar in concrete and masonry structures. The market for these anchors can be divided into three main types: polyester-based, epoxy resin-based, and other anchors. Polyester-based anchors are known for their high bond strength and fast curing time. Epoxy resin-based anchors offer superior performance in high-stress applications. Other types of anchors may include hybrid adhesives or vinyl ester-based anchors, each offering unique features and benefits for specific applications in construction projects.</p></p>
