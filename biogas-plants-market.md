<p><p>Biogas plants market types include Wet Digestion and Dry Digestion. Wet Digestion involves converting organic waste into biogas using water and bacteria in an anaerobic environment. This method is suitable for high moisture content feedstock like manure and food waste. Dry Digestion, on the other hand, is a process that converts organic waste into biogas without the use of water, making it ideal for low-moisture feedstock like agricultural residues and crop waste. Both methods offer sustainable solutions for waste management and renewable energy production.</p></p>
