<p><p>The 21700 battery pack market offers two main types: series battery pack and parallel battery pack. In a series battery pack, the cells are connected end-to-end, increasing the voltage while maintaining the same capacity. This type is ideal for applications that require higher voltage output. On the other hand, a parallel battery pack connects the cells side-by-side, keeping the voltage constant but increasing the overall capacity. This type is best suited for applications that require longer runtimes.</p></p>
