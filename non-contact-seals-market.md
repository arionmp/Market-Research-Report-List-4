<p><p>Non-contact seals market types include gap seals, labyrinth seals, gasket seals, and others. Gap seals create a small space between components to prevent contact. Labyrinth seals use a series of barriers to create a winding path for contaminants to prevent entry. Gasket seals use a flexible material to create a barrier between components. Other types of non-contact seals may include brush seals, face seals, and lip seals, each designed to prevent contact between components while maintaining a seal.</p></p>
