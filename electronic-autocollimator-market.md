<p><p>Electronic autocollimators are precision optical instruments used for measuring small angular deviations with high accuracy. They are available in two main types: without micrometers and with micrometers. Autocollimators without micrometers are used for measuring angular deviations directly without the need for additional measuring devices. Autocollimators with micrometers have integrated micrometers that allow for precise measurements of angular deviations along with the ability to make fine adjustments. Both types are essential tools in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and semiconductor for alignment and calibration tasks.</p></p>
