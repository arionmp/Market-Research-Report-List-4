<p><p>Robotic gripper systems can be classified into two main types: Electric Grippers and Pneumatic Grippers. Electric grippers are powered by electricity and offer precise and smooth gripping capabilities suitable for delicate objects. On the other hand, pneumatic grippers use compressed air to generate force for gripping objects, making them more robust and suitable for heavy-duty applications. Both types of grippers have their own advantages and are commonly used in industrial automation for various tasks such as pick and place operations.</p></p>
