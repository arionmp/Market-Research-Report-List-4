<p><p>Dental sterilization cabinetry market offers different types of sterilization methods including dry heat, steam, and bead sterilization. Dry heat sterilization uses hot air to eliminate bacteria and other organisms from instruments. Steam sterilization, also known as autoclaving, uses high-pressure steam to achieve sterilization. Bead sterilization involves heating glass beads to high temperatures for sterilizing instruments. Each method has its advantages and is used based on the specific requirements of the dental practice.</p></p>
