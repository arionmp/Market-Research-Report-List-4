<p><p>Side thrusters are devices used to provide additional maneuverability to ships and boats by pushing water in a lateral direction. The market for side thrusters can be broadly categorized into three types: tunnel side thrusters, which are installed inside the vessel's hull for minimum drag; externally mounted side thrusters, which are mounted on the outside of the hull; and waterjet side thrusters, which use a waterjet propulsion system for enhanced efficiency. Each type offers unique benefits and is suited for different types of vessels and applications.</p></p>
