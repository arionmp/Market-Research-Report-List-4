<p><ul><li>Coil Spring</li><li>Air Spring</li><li>Leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Suspension spring market is divided into three main types: coil springs, air springs, and leaf springs. Coil springs are traditional metal springs that compress and expand to absorb shocks and vibrations. Air springs use compressed air to support the vehicle's weight and provide a smooth ride. Leaf springs are made up of multiple layers of metal strips that flex under pressure. Each type of spring has its own advantages and is used in different vehicles depending on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
