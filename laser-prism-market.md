<p><ul><li>Right Angle Prism</li><li>Wedge Prism</li><li>Penta Prism</li><li>Amici Roof Prism</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser prism market consists of various types of prisms that are used in different applications. Right angle prisms are commonly used for beam steering and image erecting. Wedge prisms are utilized for beam steering without changing the image orientation. Penta prisms are used for reflections of 90 degrees and applications requiring deviation without inverting the image. Amici roof prisms are ideal for applications where a right-angle image is required without space limitations. Each type of prism offers unique advantages for specific laser applications.</p></p>
