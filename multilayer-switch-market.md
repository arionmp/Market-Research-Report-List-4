<p><strong>The Multilayer Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switch</li><li>Layer 3 Switch</li><li>Layer 4 Switch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multilayer switches are classified into three types based on the layers of the OSI model they operate at. Layer 2 switches work at the data link layer, handling Ethernet frames to route traffic within a network. Layer 3 switches operate at the network layer, providing IP routing capabilities for interconnecting different networks. Layer 4 switches work at the transport layer and can perform more advanced functions such as load balancing and traffic prioritization. Each type of switch offers different levels of functionality and performance to meet varying networking requirements.</p></p>
