<p><p>The plastic pill bottle market consists of two main types: PET (polyethylene terephthalate) and HDPE (high-density polyethylene). PET bottles are known for their clarity and durability, making them suitable for displaying colorful pills and liquids. On the other hand, HDPE bottles are preferred for their strong chemical resistance and moisture barrier properties, making them ideal for storing pharmaceutical products that require protection from outside elements. Both PET and HDPE bottles serve different purposes within the pharmaceutical industry, catering to various needs of consumers and manufacturers.</p></p>
