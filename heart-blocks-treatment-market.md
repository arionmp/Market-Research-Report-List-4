<p><strong>The Heart Blocks Treatment Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Primary Heart Blocks</li><li>Second Heart Blocks</li><li>Third Degree Heart Blocks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Heart block treatment market comprises of applications for primary (1st degree), second degree, and third degree heart blocks. Primary heart blocks occur when there is a delay in the electrical impulse between the atria and ventricles. Second degree heart blocks involve intermittent failure of electrical impulses to reach the ventricles. Third degree heart blocks are characterized by a complete blockage of electrical signals between the atria and ventricles. Various treatment options such as medication, pacemakers, and surgery are available for managing these conditions.</p></p>
