<p><strong>The Chocolate Powdered Drinks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Freeze Dried</li><li>Spray Dried</li><li>Chicory Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chocolate powdered drinks come in various market types such as freeze-dried, spray-dried, and chicory-based. Freeze-dried products are made by freezing the liquid chocolate and then removing the water through sublimation. Spray-dried drinks involve the liquid chocolate being sprayed into hot air to remove the water content. Chicory-based drinks are made from roasted chicory root mixed with chocolate powder. Each market type offers a unique taste and texture to cater to different consumer preferences in the chocolate beverage industry.</p></p>
