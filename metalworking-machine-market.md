<p><p>The metalworking machine market can be broadly classified into two types: metalforming and metalcutting. In the metalforming market, machines are used to shape metal into various forms through processes like bending, forging, and stamping. This market caters to industries like automotive, aerospace, and construction. On the other hand, the metalcutting market involves machines that remove material from metal workpieces through processes like milling, turning, and drilling. This market serves industries such as machining, fabrication, and tooling.</p></p>
