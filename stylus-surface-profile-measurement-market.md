<p><p>Stylus surface profile measurement is categorized into three main types: inductive, piezoelectric, and photoelectric. Inductive stylus systems use electromagnetic induction for detecting surface profiles, piezoelectric systems rely on the piezoelectric effect for sensing surface variations, and photoelectric systems utilize light beams to measure surface profiles. Each type has its unique benefits and applications in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing. These technologies play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and precise surface measurements for quality control and research purposes.</p></p>
