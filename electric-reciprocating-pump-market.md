<p><p>The electric reciprocating pump market consists of three main types: piston pump, plunger pump, and diaphragm pump. Piston pumps use a piston to create pressure and move fluid through the system. Plunger pumps have a solid plunger that moves back and forth to displace the fluid. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm that moves up and down to create suction and discharge pressure. Each type has its own unique characteristics and is used in various industries for different applications.</p></p>
