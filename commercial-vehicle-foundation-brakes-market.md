<p><p>The Commercial Vehicle Foundation Brakes market consists of three main types: S-Cam Drum Brakes, Disc Brakes, and Wedge Brakes. S-Cam Drum Brakes are the most common type, featuring a camshaft to push brake shoes against the drum. Disc Brakes use calipers to press brake pads against a rotor for stopping power. Wedge Brakes operate by forcing brake shoes against the brake drum using a wedge mechanism. Each type has its advantages and is commonly used in various commercial vehicles for efficient braking performance.</p></p>
