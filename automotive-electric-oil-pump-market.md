<p><p>Automotive Electric Oil Pump market types include separate pump and integrated pump. On the other hand, integrated pump systems have the electric oil pump integrated directly into the engine, saving space and simplifying installation. Each type offers unique advantages and caters to different requirements in the automotive industry, allowing manufacturers to choose the most suitable option for their vehicle designs.</p></p>
