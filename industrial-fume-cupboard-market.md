<p><p>Industrial fume cupboards are essential for managing harmful fumes and gases in workplaces. There are two common types in the market: ductless fume cupboards and ducted fume cupboards. Ductless fume cupboards use filters to clean the air, making them easy to install and move. Ducted fume cupboards, on the other hand, are connected to an external ventilation system, providing continuous removal of fumes. Both types have their advantages and are used in various industrial settings based on specific requirements.</p></p>
