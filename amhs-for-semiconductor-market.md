<p><p>Automated Material Handling Systems (AMHS) are crucial for Semiconductor Markets like Stocker (STK), Overhead Transfer (OHT), Overhead Hoist (OHS), Rail Guided Vehicle (RGV), and Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV). STK systems store and retrieve semiconductor materials efficiently. OHT and OHS systems transport materials overhead within a facility. RGV systems move materials along a fixed rail route, while AGV systems navigate autonomously on the floor. These AMHS types enhance manufacturing productivity, safety, and flexibility in semiconductor facilities.</p></p>
