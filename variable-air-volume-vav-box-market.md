<p><p>Variable Air Volume (VAV) Box market consists of several types catering to different HVAC system needs. Single-Duct Variable Air Volume systems regulate air flow in response to temperature changes in a single duct. Dual-Duct Variable Air Volume systems control air flow in two separate ducts for enhanced efficiency. Fan-Powered Variable Air Volume systems utilize fans to distribute air throughout a building. Other types of VAV boxes may include pressure-independent, bypass, or series fan powered units, providing a wide range of options for HVAC system customization.</p></p>
