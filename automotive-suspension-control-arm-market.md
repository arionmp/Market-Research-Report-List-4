<p><strong>The Automotive Suspension Control Arm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upper Arms</li><li>Lower Arms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive suspension control arms are of two types: upper arms and lower arms. Upper arms are usually shorter and connect the upper part of the wheel hub to the vehicle's body, providing vertical stability and allowing the wheel to move up and down smoothly. On the other hand, lower arms are longer and connect the lower part of the wheel hub to the vehicle's body, helping to control the wheel's horizontal movement. Both upper and lower control arms are crucial components in ensuring a smooth and stable ride for the vehicle.</p></p>
