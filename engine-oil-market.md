<p><p>Engine oil market consists of mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from crude oil through a refining process, providing basic lubrication and protection for engines. Synthetic oil is manufactured using chemically modified petroleum components or substances like polyalphaolefins, offering enhanced performance and protection compared to mineral oil. Synthetic oil is known for its superior temperature resistance, oxidation stability, and longer lifespan. Both mineral and synthetic oil markets cater to various consumer needs based on engine type, usage, and performance requirements.</p></p>
