<p><p>Vacuum breakers are categorized into two types: low pressure and high pressure. Low pressure vacuum breakers are designed for systems operating under lower pressures, typically below 25 psi. They are commonly used in residential settings and are simpler in design. On the other hand, high pressure vacuum breakers are suited for systems with higher pressures, usually above 25 psi. They are more complex and sturdy in construction, making them suitable for industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
