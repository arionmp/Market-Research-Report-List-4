<p><p>Commercial PV systems are classified into two main market types based on the types of solar panels used: monocrystalline systems and polysilicon systems. Monocrystalline systems use solar panels made from a single crystal structure, providing higher efficiency and energy output. Polysilicon systems, on the other hand, use solar panels made from multiple silicon crystals, offering a more cost-effective alternative. Both types of systems are popular choices for commercial applications, with each offering unique benefits depending on the specific needs and preferences of the user.</p></p>
