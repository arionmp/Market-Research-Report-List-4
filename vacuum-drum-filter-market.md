<p><p>A Vacuum Drum Filter is a type of filter that uses vacuum pressure to separate solid particles from a liquid. The filter consists of a rotating drum covered with filter cloth, which is immersed in the liquid to be filtered. As the drum rotates, the vacuum pressure inside the drum draws the liquid through the filter cloth, trapping the solid particles on the surface of the drum.</p><p>The Vacuum Drum Filter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period.
