<p><p>Steel mill skimmers are essential for removing slag and impurities from steel during the manufacturing process. Two common types in the market are belt type and disc type skimmers. Belt type skimmers use a conveyor belt to collect and remove impurities from the surface of the molten steel, while disc type skimmers use rotating discs for the same purpose. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the quality and purity of the steel produced in a mill.</p></p>
