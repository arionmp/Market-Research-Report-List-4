<p><p>The Two Wheelers Automotive EVAP Systems market includes various components such as Fuel Tank, Gas Cap, Liquid Vapor Separator, Control Valve, Sensor, and Hoses. The Fuel Tank is where the vapor is stored, while the Gas Cap prevents fuel from evaporating. The Liquid Vapor Separator separates liquid fuel from vapor. The Control Valve regulates the flow of vapor, while the Sensor detects any issues in the system. Hoses connect these components and allow for the transfer of vapor within the system.</p></p>
