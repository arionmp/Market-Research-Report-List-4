<p><p>Pipe threading machines come in two main types - manual and automatic. Manual machines require the operator to manually turn a handle to thread the pipe, making them suitable for small scale applications and occasional use. In contrast, automatic machines are more advanced, featuring motorized operation that significantly reduces the physical effort required. These machines are ideal for heavy-duty industrial applications where high volume and precision threading is needed. Each type caters to different needs and preferences based on the scale and frequency of pipe threading required.</p></p>
