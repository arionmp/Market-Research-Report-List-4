<p><p>Pneumatic nailers are air-powered tools used for fastening nails into various materials. There are two main types of pneumatic nailers on the market: coil-style and strip-style. Coil-style nailers use a coil of nails, allowing for a larger nail capacity and less frequent reloading. Strip-style nailers use a long strip of nails that are loaded into a straight magazine. Both types are popular in construction and carpentry for their efficiency and ease of use.</p></p>
