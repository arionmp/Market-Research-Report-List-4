<p><strong>The Steering Wheel ECUs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>REPS</li><li>CEPS</li><li>PEPS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steering Wheel ECUs can be classified into three main types: REPS (Resistor Electric Power Steering), CEPS (Column Electric Power Steering), and PEPS (Pinion Electric Power Steering). REPS systems use a torque sensor on the steering column to provide power assistance, while CEPS systems have the sensor built into the steering column itself. PEPS systems utilize a pinion motor to provide power assistance. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
