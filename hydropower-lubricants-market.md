<p><p>Hydropower lubricants are divided into two main types based on their applications: heavy duty and light duty. Heavy duty hydropower lubricants are specifically designed for high-pressure and high-temperature applications, such as hydraulic turbines and generators. They provide superior protection and performance under extreme conditions. On the other hand, light duty hydropower lubricants are suitable for lower pressure and temperature applications, such as valves and control systems. They offer adequate lubrication and protection for less demanding hydropower equipment.</p></p>
