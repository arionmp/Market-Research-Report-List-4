<p><p>GNSS antennas are categorized into two types: internal and external. Internal antennas are integrated within a device, such as a smartphone or a tablet, providing a compact and convenient solution for users. While internal antennas are more common in consumer devices, external antennas are preferred in applications where precise positioning is crucial, such as in surveying or agriculture.</p></p>
