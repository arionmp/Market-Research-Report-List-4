Airframe Heavy Maintenance involves the inspection and repair of the helicopter's main structure, including the fuselage, landing gear, and rotor system. Engine Maintenance focuses on the inspection and repair of the helicopter's engines to ensure optimal performance. Component Maintenance involves the inspection and repair of specific components such as avionics, hydraulics, and electrical systems to ensure the overall functionality of the helicopter.</p></p>
