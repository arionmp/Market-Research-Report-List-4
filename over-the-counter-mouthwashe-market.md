<p><p>Over-the-counter mouthwash is available in two main types: alcoholic and non-alcoholic. Alcoholic mouthwashes typically contain substances like ethanol or other alcohols, which can provide a tingling sensation and offer antibacterial properties. Non-alcoholic mouthwashes, on the other hand, use alternative ingredients like cetylpyridinium chloride or chlorine dioxide to achieve similar benefits without the strong sensation or taste of alcohol. Both types of mouthwash can help freshen breath, fight plaque and bacteria, and promote overall oral health.</p></p>
