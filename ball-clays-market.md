<p><ul><li>20-50% Kaolinite Ball Clays</li><li>50-80% Kaolinite Ball Clays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ball clays are classified based on their kaolinite content. Ball clays containing 20-50% kaolinite are known as low kaolinite ball clays, while those with 50-80% kaolinite are classified as high kaolinite ball clays. Low kaolinite ball clays are valued for their plasticity and workability, making them ideal for use in ceramics. On the other hand, high kaolinite ball clays are preferred for their higher firing temperature and strength, making them suitable for various industrial applications such as refractories and construction materials.</p></p>
