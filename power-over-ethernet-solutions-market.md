<p><p>Power over Ethernet (PoE) solutions market consists of two main types: Power Sourcing Equipment (PSE) Controllers & ICs and Powered Device (PD) Controllers & ICs. PSE Controllers & ICs are devices that provide power to the network, such as switches or injectors. On the other hand, PD Controllers & ICs are devices that receive power from the network, such as IP cameras or access points. These components are essential in PoE systems to ensure efficient power delivery and communication between devices.</p></p>
