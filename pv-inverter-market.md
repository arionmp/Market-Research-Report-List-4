<p><p>PV inverters are essential components of solar energy systems, converting DC electricity produced by solar panels into AC electricity usable in homes and businesses. The three main types of PV inverters are string inverters, central inverters, and microinverters. String inverters are cost-effective and commonly used for small to medium-sized installations. Central inverters are suitable for large-scale projects, offering high efficiency and performance. Microinverters, on the other hand, are installed on each individual solar panel, providing flexibility and optimizing energy production.</p></p>
