<p><p>EV Charging Stations market includes two main types: AC Charging Stations and DC Charging Stations. AC Charging Stations use alternating current to charge the electric vehicle's battery, typically found in homes or workplaces where vehicles are parked for longer durations. DC Charging Stations, on the other hand, use direct current for fast charging at public locations like highways or commercial spaces. Both types cater to different needs of electric vehicle owners, providing convenient charging solutions for a growing market segment.</p></p>
