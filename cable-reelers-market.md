<p><p>Cable reelers are available in three main types: spring driven, motor driven, and manual. Spring driven cable reelers are operated by a coiled spring mechanism, which automatically retracts the cable. Motor driven cable reelers are powered by an electric motor, offering consistent and controlled cable winding. Manual cable reelers require physical effort to wind and unwind the cable. Each type of cable reeler has its own advantages and is suitable for different applications based on the level of automation and convenience required.</p></p>
