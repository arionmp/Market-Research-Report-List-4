<p><p>Wheel aligner equipment market can be categorized into three types: CCD aligner, 3D aligner, and others. CCD aligner uses cameras to measure the alignment angles of wheels and is popular due to its accuracy and ease of use. 3D aligner utilizes advanced technology to provide precise measurements in three dimensions, offering increased accuracy over traditional aligners. Other types of aligners include laser aligners and sensor-based aligners, each with their own unique measurement methods and features.</p></p>
