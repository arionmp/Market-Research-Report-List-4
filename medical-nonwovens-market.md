<p><p>Medical nonwovens market is segmented into Drylaid, Airlaid, Wetlaid, and Spunlaid types. Drylaid nonwovens are produced by mechanical bonding of fibers without using water, Airlaid nonwovens involve air as a medium for laying fibers, Wetlaid nonwovens use water for laying fibers, and Spunlaid nonwovens involve extruding molten polymer to form fibers. Each type offers distinct characteristics and applications in medical products like surgical gowns, face masks, wound dressings, and hygiene products.</p></p>
