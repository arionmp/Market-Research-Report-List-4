<p><p>Deep reactive ion etching (DRIE) systems are used in two main market types: Bosch Process and Cryo Process. The Bosch Process involves alternating high etch and passivation steps, resulting in very high aspect ratio features. On the other hand, the Cryo Process utilizes low-temperature environments to improve etch rates and profile control. Both processes are used in applications requiring high precision and deep etching capabilities, such as MEMS devices, semiconductors, and sensors. The market for DRIE systems continues to grow due to the increasing demand for advanced microfabrication technologies.</p></p>
