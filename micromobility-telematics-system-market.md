<p><p>The Micromobility Telematics System market consists of three main types: Embedded, Tethered, and Integrated. Embedded systems are built directly into the vehicle, providing real-time data without the need for additional devices. Tethered systems require a separate device to be connected to the vehicle for data collection and analysis. Integrated systems combine multiple sensors and devices to provide a seamless and comprehensive telematics solution. Each type offers unique advantages and capabilities for managing and optimizing micromobility services.</p></p>
