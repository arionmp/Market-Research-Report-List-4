<p><strong>The E Chuck for Wafer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulomb Type</li><li>Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>E Chuck for Wafer Market Types are commonly categorized as Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type chuck relies on electrostatic attraction to hold the wafer in place, while JR Type chuck utilizes a combination of electrostatic force and mechanical clamping. Coulomb Type is effective for lightweight wafers with smooth surfaces, while JR Type is preferred for heavier wafers and those with rough surfaces. Both types are essential in the semiconductor industry for securely holding wafers during processing.</p></p>
