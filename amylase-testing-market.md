<p><strong>The Amylase Testing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blood Sample Testing</li><li>Urine Sample Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amylase testing can be conducted using either blood or urine samples. Blood sample testing involves drawing a sample of blood from the patient's vein and measuring the levels of amylase enzyme present. Urine sample testing, on the other hand, involves collecting a urine sample and analyzing the levels of amylase enzyme excreted by the body. Both types of testing are commonly used to diagnose conditions related to the pancreas, such as pancreatitis, and to monitor treatment effectiveness.</p></p>
