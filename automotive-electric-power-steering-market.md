<p><p>Automotive Electric Power Steering systems can be categorized into different types based on their design and functionality. The types include Column Assist EPS, which utilizes an electric motor mounted on the steering column to assist with steering, Single-Pinion Assist EPS, which uses a single electric motor to provide steering assistance, Dual-Pinion Assist EPS, which utilizes two electric motors for improved performance, and Rack Assist EPS, which has an electric motor directly integrated into the steering rack for more precise control. Each type offers unique features and benefits for improved handling and comfort in vehicles.</p></p>
