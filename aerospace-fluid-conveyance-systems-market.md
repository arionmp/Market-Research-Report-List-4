<p><p>The Aerospace Fluid Conveyance Systems market consists of three main types - Hoses, Low-Pressure Ducts, and High-Pressure Ducts. Hoses are flexible tubes used to convey fluids in aircraft systems. Low-Pressure Ducts are rigid tubes that convey fluids at lower pressure levels. High-Pressure Ducts are also rigid tubes but are designed to handle fluids at high pressure levels. These components play a vital role in ensuring the proper flow of fluids throughout an aircraft's systems, contributing to its overall performance and safety.</p></p>
