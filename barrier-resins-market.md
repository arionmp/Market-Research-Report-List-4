<p><p>Barrier resins are used in packaging materials to provide protection against oxygen, moisture, and other external factors that can degrade the product. The main types of barrier resins in the market are EVOH resin, PVDC, PEN, and others. EVOH resin is known for its excellent oxygen barrier properties, while PVDC offers superior moisture resistance. PEN resins are used for their high clarity and good thermal stability. Other barrier resins may include nylon, PET, and metalized films, each offering unique barrier properties for different packaging needs.</p></p>
