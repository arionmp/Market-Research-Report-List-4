<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene (BOPE) Film Market includes monolayer film and composite film types. Monolayer film consists of a single layer of BOPE film, offering a basic and cost-effective packaging solution. On the other hand, composite film combines BOPE film with other materials to provide enhanced performance, such as increased barrier properties or durability. Both types cater to different packaging needs, with monolayer film being suitable for simple applications and composite film offering more advanced functionalities.</p></p>
