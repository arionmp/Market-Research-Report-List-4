<p><strong>The Automatic Calorimeter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Differential scanning calorimeter</li><li>Oxygen bomb calorimeter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automatic calorimeter market consists of different types, including the differential scanning calorimeter and oxygen bomb calorimeter. The differential scanning calorimeter measures the heat flow in a sample as a function of temperature, providing information on phase transitions and thermal properties. On the other hand, the oxygen bomb calorimeter measures the heat released during the combustion of a sample in an oxygen-rich environment, useful for determining the calorific value of fuels and food products. Both types serve important roles in various industries for analyzing thermal properties and energy content of materials.</p></p>
