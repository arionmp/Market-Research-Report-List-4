<p><p>Anaesthetic machines are available in two main types - fixed and mobile. Fixed machines are typically found in hospital operating rooms and are permanently installed for regular use. They are designed to provide a stable and consistent flow of anesthesia gases during surgical procedures. Mobile machines, on the other hand, are portable units that can be easily transported between different locations. They are commonly used in ambulatory surgery centers, emergency rooms, and other healthcare settings where mobility is required. Both types of machines play a crucial role in providing safe and effective anesthesia to patients in various medical settings.</p></p>
