<p><p>The foreign exchange market consists of various types of trading mechanisms, including Currency Swaps, Outright Forward and FX Swaps, and FX Options. Currency Swaps involve exchanging principal and interest payments in different currencies. Outright Forward and FX Swaps are agreements to buy or sell currencies at a specified future date and price. FX Options involve the right to buy or sell a currency at a predetermined exchange rate on or before a specified date. These market types provide flexibility for managing currency risk and investment opportunities.</p></p>
