<p><p>MTRJ Fiber Optic Connector is available in two market types: Singlemode and Multimode. Singlemode connectors are designed for long-distance communication with a narrow core size, allowing light to travel straight down the fiber without bouncing off the edges. They are ideal for applications requiring high bandwidth and speed. Multimode connectors have a larger core size, allowing multiple modes of light to travel through the fiber, making them suitable for shorter distance applications where high power is not required.</p></p>
