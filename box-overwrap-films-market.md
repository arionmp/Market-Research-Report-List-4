<p><p>Box overwrap films are commonly categorized into two main types: shrink films and stretch films. Shrink films are heat-sensitive films that shrink tightly around boxes when heated, providing a secure and streamlined packaging solution. Stretch films, on the other hand, are highly elastic films that are stretched and wrapped around boxes to provide stability and protection during transportation. Both types of films serve important roles in the packaging industry, catering to different needs and preferences of manufacturers and consumers.</p></p>
