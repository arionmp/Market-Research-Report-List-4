<p><p>The Nano Screen Protector market consists of two main types: Granular Film and Dense Film. Granular Film screen protectors are made up of tiny granules that help absorb impact and provide scratch resistance, offering basic protection for devices. On the other hand, Dense Film screen protectors are made of a thicker, denser material that provides more comprehensive protection against scratches, cracks, and impacts. While Granular Film protectors are more budget-friendly, Dense Film protectors offer higher levels of protection but come at a higher cost.</p></p>
