In the MAN (Metropolitan Area Network) market, IBN can streamline network management for interconnected networks within a city. In the LAN (Local Area Network) market, IBN can enhance network efficiency and security within a single building or campus. Lastly, in the PAN (Personal Area Network) market, IBN can provide personalized network access and security for individual users or devices.</p></p>
