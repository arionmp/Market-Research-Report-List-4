<p><p>Returnable Asset Monitoring involves tracking and managing assets that are reused multiple times in supply chains. Barcodes tags are the most commonly used type, providing basic identification and tracking information. RFID tags use radio waves to automatically identify and track assets, offering real-time data visibility. GPS tags go a step further by providing location tracking capabilities for assets in transit. Each type offers unique advantages in terms of cost, accuracy, and data visibility, allowing companies to better manage their returnable assets.</p></p>
