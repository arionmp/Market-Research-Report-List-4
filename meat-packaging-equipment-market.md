<p><p>The meat packaging equipment market offers various types of machines to help in the packaging process. Vacuum chamber packaging machines create a vacuum inside a chamber to securely seal the meat. Stretch film wrapping machines tightly wrap meat products in plastic film. Form/fill/seal packaging machines form packages, fill them with meat, and seal them all in one process. Tray sealers securely seal meat trays. Other types of equipment include skin packaging machines, vertical form fill seal machines, and modified atmosphere packaging machines.</p></p>
