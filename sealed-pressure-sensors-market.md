<p><p>Sealed pressure sensors are classified into three main types based on the pressure range they can measure: low pressure (up to 50 psi), mid pressure (50-500 psi), and high pressure (above 500 psi). Low pressure sealed pressure sensors are used in applications where precision in measuring small pressure differentials is crucial. Mid pressure sealed pressure sensors are commonly used in industrial applications such as hydraulic systems. High pressure sealed pressure sensors are designed for extreme environments like aerospace and oil & gas industries where high pressures need to be accurately monitored.</p></p>
