<p><p>Touchscreen Display Module market can be classified into two main types: Capacitive Touchscreen Display Module and Resistive Touchscreen Display Module. </p><p>Capacitive touchscreen display modules use electrical properties of the human body to detect touch, resulting in a more sensitive and durable touch experience. On the other hand, Resistive touchscreen display modules detect touch by pressure, making them suitable for use with gloves or styluses. Each type has its own advantages and are used in various applications based on their specific functionalities and requirements in the market.</p></p>
