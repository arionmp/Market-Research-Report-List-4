<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Ultrasound Imaging System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A- Scan</li><li>B- Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymeter</li><li>Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ophthalmic ultrasound imaging systems are used for diagnostic imaging of the eyes. A-scan technology measures the length of the eye and helps in diagnosing conditions like cataracts and glaucoma. B-scan provides a two-dimensional view of the eye and is useful for detecting retinal detachments or tumors. Combined scan integrates both A-scan and B-scan capabilities. Pachymeter measures the thickness of the cornea. Ultrasound bio-microscopy (UBM) offers detailed images of the anterior segment of the eye, aiding in diagnosing conditions like tumors and detachments.</p></p>
