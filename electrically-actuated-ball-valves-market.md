<p><p>Electrically actuated ball valves are available in various types based on the connection method. Threaded connections are commonly used for smaller sizes and low-pressure applications. Flanged connections are suitable for larger sizes and high-pressure applications. Butt weld connections are used for high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Socket weld connections are ideal for smaller sizes and are easier to install. Each type of connection offers different strengths and limitations depending on the intended use in various industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p></p>
