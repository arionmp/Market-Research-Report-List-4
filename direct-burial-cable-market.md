<p><strong>The Direct Burial Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Twisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct burial cables are cables specifically designed for burial underground without the need for conduit. There are two main types of direct burial cables: solid and twisted. Solid direct burial cables consist of a single conductor, providing a simple and efficient option for installations. Twisted direct burial cables, on the other hand, have multiple conductors twisted together, offering improved signal integrity and resistance to interference. Both types are commonly used in outdoor applications where traditional cables may not be suitable due to exposure to the elements.</p></p>
