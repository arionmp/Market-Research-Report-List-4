<p><p>Precast construction market types include floors & roofs, walls & barriers, columns & beams, utility vaults, girders, pipes, and paving slabs. Floors & roofs are precast elements that provide structural support and a finished surface for buildings. Walls & barriers are precast panels used for dividing spaces or providing security. Columns & beams are vertical and horizontal structural elements that support the weight of a building. Utility vaults are precast chambers for housing underground utilities. Girders are horizontal beams used in bridges and other structures. Pipes are precast conduits for conveying fluids. Paving slabs are precast units used for pedestrian or vehicular paving applications.</p></p>
