<p><p>Wood fillers are used to repair and fill cracks, holes, and imperfections in wood surfaces. There are two main types of wood fillers based on their composition: solvent-based and water-based. Solvent-based fillers contain chemicals that can emit strong odors and require proper ventilation during use. On the other hand, water-based fillers are non-toxic, easy to clean up with water, and have low odor, making them more environmentally friendly. Both types of wood fillers provide durability and can be sanded, stained, and painted after drying.</p></p>
