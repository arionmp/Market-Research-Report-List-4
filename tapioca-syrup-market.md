<p><p>Tapioca syrup market includes various types such as tapioca syrup, tapioca maltodextrins, and tapioca syrup solids. Tapioca syrup is a sweetener derived from the cassava root, commonly used in food and beverage products. Tapioca maltodextrins are produced from tapioca syrup and are used as a thickening agent in food products. Tapioca syrup solids are dried forms of tapioca syrup and are often used as a binding agent or sweetener in food manufacturing. Each of these products caters to different needs within the food industry.</p></p>
