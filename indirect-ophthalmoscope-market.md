In Peripheral Neovascularization, they aid in examining abnormal blood vessel growth at the back of the eye. In Pediatric Retinal Repairs, they assist in treating retinal disorders in children. In Lattice Degeneration, they are used to monitor and manage retinal thinning and holes in the eye.</p></p>
