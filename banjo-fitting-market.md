<p><p>Banjo fittings come in several different types to suit various plumbing and hydraulic system requirements. Linear banjo fittings are used for straight connections, while elbow banjo fittings are ideal for connections at right angles. T-shaped banjo fittings are used to split a single fluid line into two separate lines. Y-shaped banjo fittings are designed to merge two fluid lines into a single line. Each type of banjo fitting serves a specific purpose in the efficient functioning of plumbing and hydraulic systems.</p></p>
