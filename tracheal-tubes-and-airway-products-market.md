<p><p>Tracheal tubes and airway products market comprises various types of products such as Endotracheal Tubes, Tracheostomy Tubes, Breathing Circuits, Oral and Nasopharyngeal Airways, and Laryngeal masks. Endotracheal Tubes are inserted into the trachea through the mouth or nose to maintain an open airway during surgery or respiratory failure. Tracheostomy Tubes are placed directly into the trachea through a surgical incision in the neck. Breathing circuits provide a pathway for delivering oxygen and removing carbon dioxide. Oral and Nasopharyngeal Airways help maintain an open airway during medical procedures. Laryngeal masks are inserted into the larynx to facilitate ventilation during surgeries.</p></p>
