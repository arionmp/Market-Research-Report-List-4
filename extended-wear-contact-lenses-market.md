<p><strong>The Extended Wear Contact Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spherical Form</li><li>Toric Form</li><li>Multifocal Form</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Extended wear contact lenses are available in three main types: spherical form, toric form, and multifocal form. Spherical form lenses are designed for individuals with nearsightedness or farsightedness. Toric form lenses are specially made for individuals with astigmatism, providing clear vision by correcting the irregular curvature of the cornea. Multifocal form lenses are designed to correct presbyopia, allowing individuals to see clearly at both near and far distances. Each type of extended wear contact lens caters to specific vision needs, providing convenient and effective vision correction options.</p></p>
