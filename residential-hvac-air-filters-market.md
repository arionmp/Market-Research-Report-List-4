<p><p>Residential HVAC Air Filters market includes two main types: Pleated HVAC Air Filters and Panel HVAC Air Filters. Pleated HVAC Air Filters are constructed with layered, pleated materials that provide a larger surface area for capturing dust and allergens. They are more efficient in capturing smaller particles. On the other hand, Panel HVAC Air Filters are made of flat, fibrous material that filters out larger particles, making them more economical but less effective in capturing tiny particles. Both types are essential for maintaining clean and healthy indoor air quality.</p></p>
