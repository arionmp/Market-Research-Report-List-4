<p><strong>The Geophone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric Geophones</li><li>Piezoelectric Geophones</li><li>Eddy Current Geophones</li><li>Digital Geophone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Geophones are seismic sensors used in geophysical exploration to detect ground vibrations. The market for geophones can be classified into different types based on the technology used. Electric geophones use electromagnetic induction to convert ground vibrations into electrical signals. Piezoelectric geophones utilize piezoelectric materials to convert mechanical stress into electrical signals. Eddy current geophones sense ground vibrations through changes in magnetic fields. The digital geophone market refers to geophones that have digital signal processing capabilities, offering improved data accuracy and storage capability.</p></p>
